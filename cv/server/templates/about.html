What stops you from exercising?

Battling gym anxiety is a struggle new gym-goers often face. Studies show that 15% of people experience anxiety when exercising at a public gym. The journey to an active and healthy lifestyle should not involve crowded gyms and judgment from other gym-goers, and that’s why we invented Phormatics. Phormatics allows you to exercise at the comfort of your own home with an intelligent AI system that tracks your daily progress and builds an individualized workout system just for you!

Exercising can cause serious physical damage if not performed using proper techniques and forms. Phormatics, however, uses an intelligent computer vision system which constantly tracks your movements, ensuring your form is perfect. Not only has your workout become that much more efficient, it also limits your risk of injury. As long as you have a camera on an Phormatics-compatible desktop/mobile device, you can be sure that every exercise will bring you closer to the healthy lifestyle you've always wanted.

We\’re focused on giving a revolutionary exercise experience for every individual, no judgement involved. We understand that sometimes there just isn/’t enough time to go to the gym or build a perfect exercise routine. With Phormatics, you can exercise in the convenience of your own home with an intelligent AI bot building the ideal routine.

Everyone can benefit from a gym buddy, or Gymmies, as we call them. A simple Facebook login, and you are connected to various Gymmies around you, creating a community of fitness activists. We want to make sure that even from home, you are never working out alone. Congratulate others on their fitness accomplishments by giving them a "spot", share new routines and see what you can do to make your community more active. We've throughly enjoyed developing and finetuning Phormatics and hope you share in our vision of using AI to transform your fitness/social life forever.
